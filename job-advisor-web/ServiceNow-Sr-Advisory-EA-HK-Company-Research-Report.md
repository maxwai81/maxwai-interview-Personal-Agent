# Company Research Report: ServiceNow

*Context: Sr Advisory Enterprise Architect — Hong Kong (Causeway Bay), pre-sales / advisory architecture*

---

## Executive Summary

ServiceNow is a global enterprise workflow and AI platform (NYSE: NOW) that positions the **Now Platform** as a unified “platform of platforms” for IT, employee, and customer workflows. The **Sr Advisory Enterprise Architect** role in **Hong Kong** sits in pre-sales, partnering with **CIOs, CTOs, and CDOs** across **APAC** to design enterprise-grade architectures, lead discovery and workshops, and align ServiceNow roadmaps with customer transformation goals. The posting emphasizes **enterprise architecture frameworks** (e.g., TOGAF, Zachman, IT4IT, BIAN, TMForum), **integration** across hybrid and legacy estates, **cloud/SaaS/PaaS** fluency, and **AI** applied to customer and internal problem-solving. The Hong Kong base (Causeway Bay, flexible persona) supports strategic accounts in the region alongside Account Executives, Solution Consultants, partners, and Professional Services.

## Recommendation for candidates

**Verdict:** **Strong employer / role** for senior architects who thrive in **pre-sales**, **executive storytelling**, and **platform-level** design—especially with **integration** and **governance** depth. **Hong Kong** base is credible for **APAC** enterprise accounts. Success requires fluency in **ServiceNow’s platform story**, **customer EA standards**, and **multi-stakeholder** sales cycles.

**Questions to ask interviewers**
1. How does the HK Advisory EA team prioritize **accounts and industries** in APAC, and how is quota/coverage shared with SCs and partners?
2. Which **reference architectures** or **industry patterns** are most used in HK/Greater China wins?
3. How is **AI** positioned in enterprise architecture engagements today (governance, agent patterns, data residency)?
4. What does **90-day success** look like—pipeline contribution, customer workshops, or specific certifications?

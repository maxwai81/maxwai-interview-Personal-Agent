# Company Research Report: ServiceNow

*Applying for Director Solution Consulting Hong Kong in Hong Kong (HQ: Santa Clara, CA)*

---

## Executive Summary

ServiceNow is a leading enterprise cloud platform company (~$13.3B revenue, ~29,000 employees) focused on workflow automation and AI. The company has a strong Hong Kong presence as North Asia regional headquarters, with offices in Causeway Bay and Wan Chai. **Eddy Chan** leads Solution Consulting for North Asia (Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan), and Director-level roles exist in the APAC solution consulting structure. Glassdoor ratings are strong (4.3/5, 85% recommend, 95% CEO approval). The Director Solution Consulting role focuses on leading and scaling pre-sales teams, technical discovery, POCs, and capability building across North Asia. Recent news includes the $2.85B Moveworks acquisition (March 2025), reinforcing ServiceNow’s AI-first strategy.

---

## Company Background

- **Mission:** "Make the world work better for everyone"
- **Purpose:** "Making AI Work for People and Business" / "Put AI to Work"
- **Founded:** November 5, 2003 by Fred Luddy as Glidesoft, Inc. in Santa Clara, CA
- **History:** Luddy was CTO at Peregrine Systems; started alone, grew with volunteers; renamed to Service-Now.com in 2006; IPO June 2012 ($210M); HQ moved from San Diego to Santa Clara
- **Values:** People Pact – "do our best work, live our best lives, and fulfill our purpose together"; humility and integrity; confidence without arrogance; customer-first; collaborative culture
- **Scale:** ~29,187 employees (2025); ~$13.28B revenue (2025); NYSE: NOW; S&P 100 and S&P 500
- **Industry:** Enterprise software / cloud computing

## Products & Services

### Platform
ServiceNow AI Platform (cloud-native PaaS) for workflow automation, orchestration, and app development across the enterprise.

### Core Offerings
- **AI & automation:** AI Agents, AI Experience, AI Control Tower, Autonomous Workforce, Now Assist
- **IT:** IT Service Management (ITIL-aligned), IT Operations Management, IT Asset Management
- **Service delivery:** HR Service Delivery, Customer Service Management, Field Service Management, Workplace Service Delivery
- **Business operations:** Strategic Portfolio Management, App Engine, Procurement/Source-to-Pay, Legal Service Delivery
- **Data & integration:** Workflow Data Fabric, RaptorDB
- **Security:** Security Operations, Third-party Risk Management, Vault & Platform Encryption

### Solution Consulting Context
Solution Consulting (pre-sales) includes Solution Architect, Solution Engineer, and Advisory Solution Consultant roles. Director-level roles lead and scale teams; focus areas: pre-sales, technical discovery, POCs, capability building, and mentoring.

## Director Solution Consulting Hong Kong – Role-Specific Findings

### Hong Kong Office & Structure
- **Head of Solution Consulting, North Asia:** Eddy Chan (Hong Kong) – Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan
- **Director, Partner Solution Consulting – APAC:** Remote/hybrid; leads APAC partner solution consulting
- **Solution Sales Executive, Core Business Solutions (Hong Kong):** Causeway Bay/Wan Chai; North Asia (HK, Macau, Taiwan, Philippines)
- **Advisory Solution Consultant:** Hong Kong-based; focus accounts, subject matter expertise, mentoring

### Director Solution Consulting Context
- Director roles lead and scale solution consulting teams across North Asia
- Focus: pre-sales, technical discovery, POCs, capability building, team enablement
- APAC/North Asia structure supports a Director Solution Consulting Hong Kong role
- Likely reporting to regional or global Solution Consulting leadership (e.g., Eddy Chan or APAC-level leadership)

### Interview Preparation Tips (Director-Level)
1. **AI strategy:** Emphasize experience with AI-powered workflows, agentic AI, and enterprise platforms; understand Moveworks integration
2. **People Pact:** Connect your leadership values to "do our best work, live our best lives"
3. **North Asia focus:** Highlight Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and cross-border experience; familiarity with local enterprise buyers
4. **Product depth:** ITSM, workflow automation, Now Platform, Now Assist, Core Business Solutions
5. **Leadership:** Research Bill McDermott and Amit Zavery; mention Eddy Chan for North Asia context
6. **Team scaling:** Prepare examples of building and scaling pre-sales teams, capability programs, and mentoring

## Recommendation

**Verdict:** **Good** — ServiceNow is a strong employer with a clear Hong Kong presence, competitive ratings, and a Director Solution Consulting role that fits the North Asia structure. Eddy Chan leads Solution Consulting for the region, and the company’s AI-first strategy (Moveworks, partnerships) aligns with growth and innovation.

**Key factors:**
- Hong Kong as North Asia HQ (Causeway Bay, Wan Chai) with established Solution Consulting leadership
- Director-level roles exist in APAC/North Asia; clear path for leadership in pre-sales
- Strong Glassdoor ratings (4.3/5, 85% recommend) and high CEO approval
- Recent AI investments (Moveworks $2.85B, partnerships) indicate growth and innovation
- Interview process is moderate difficulty with clear focus on discovery, solution design, and leadership

**Questions to ask in the interview:**
1. How does the Director Solution Consulting Hong Kong role fit within the North Asia structure, and what is the reporting line to Eddy Chan or APAC leadership?
2. What does success look like for a Director in the first 12 months—team size, key accounts, and capability-building priorities?
3. How does ServiceNow support remote or hybrid work for Hong Kong-based Directors, and what is the typical mix of local vs. regional (Macau, Taiwan) engagements?
4. How is the Moveworks acquisition changing the Solution Consulting playbook and AI demos for North Asia customers?

## 1. Executive Summary

**AIA Group Limited** and its subsidiaries form the **largest independent publicly listed pan-Asian life insurance group**, with a presence across **18 markets** including Hong Kong SAR and Macau SAR. Originating in Shanghai (1919), the Group is a **market leader in Asia (ex-Japan)** by life insurance premiums and reports **total assets of approximately US$328 billion** (Group disclosure, mid-2025). AIA serves **tens of millions** of individual policyholders and group scheme members through agents, partners, and digital channels.

**AIA Hong Kong** positions itself around **trust, leadership, and digital customer engagement**: public messaging highlights **No. 1 in new business policies** (multi-year streak), **Premier Agency** and **MDRT / COT / TOT** production leadership, and the **AIA+** mobile app as a core digital touchpoint. The **Principal — Agency Digital & Innovation** role sits in the **agency distribution** engine—driving **digital agent engagement**, **analytics**, **platform evolution**, and **innovation** aligned with regulatory and compliance expectations in Hong Kong’s insurance market.

**Candidate relevance:** The role rewards leaders who can **industrialize digital strategies** for agents, **partner across IT and business**, and **scale adoption** of digital capabilities—context where enterprise **SaaS, data, and transformation** experience transfers, while **life insurance agency operations and local regulatory nuance** require deliberate upskilling.

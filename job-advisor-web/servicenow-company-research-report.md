# Company Research Report: ServiceNow

*Applying for Advisory Solution Consultant, Core Business Solutions in Hong Kong (HQ: Santa Clara, CA)*

---

## Executive Summary

ServiceNow is a leading enterprise cloud platform company (~$13.3B revenue, ~29,000 employees) focused on workflow automation and AI. The company has a strong Hong Kong presence with regional offices in Causeway Bay and Wan Chai, and offers remote and hybrid work for APAC roles. The Advisory Solution Consultant, Core Business Solutions role aligns with ServiceNow’s back-office solutions (HR, legal, procurement, finance). Glassdoor ratings are strong (4.3/5, 85% recommend), and the interview process for Solution Consultants is rated moderate (3/5 difficulty) with emphasis on discovery, solution design, and stakeholder management.

### Platform
ServiceNow AI Platform (cloud-native PaaS) for workflow automation, orchestration, and app development across the enterprise.

### Core Offerings
- **AI & automation:** AI Agents, AI Experience, AI Control Tower, Autonomous Workforce
- **IT:** IT Service Management, IT Operations Management, IT Asset Management
- **Service delivery:** HR Service Delivery, Customer Service Management, Field Service Management, Workplace Service Delivery
- **Business operations:** Strategic Portfolio Management, App Engine, Procurement/Source-to-Pay, Legal Service Delivery

- **Security:** Security Operations, Third-party Risk Management, Vault & Platform Encryption

### Core Business Solutions / Core Business Suite (Role-Relevant)
Back-office and shared services (HR, legal, procurement, finance) delivered on the ServiceNow platform. "Now on Now" is ServiceNow's own use of these solutions internally. This is the product area for the Advisory Solution Consultant, Core Business Solutions role.

## Recommendation

**Verdict:** **Good** — ServiceNow is a strong employer with a clear Hong Kong presence, competitive ratings, and a role that aligns with Core Business Solutions. The Advisory Solution Consultant position fits well for candidates with discovery, solution design, and stakeholder management experience.

**Key factors:**
- Hong Kong regional HQ (Causeway Bay, Wan Chai) with remote and hybrid options for APAC
- Core Business Solutions (HR, legal, procurement, finance) is a strategic product area
- Strong Glassdoor ratings (4.3/5, 85% recommend) and high CEO approval
- Recent AI investments (Moveworks, partnerships) indicate growth and innovation
- Interview process is moderate difficulty with clear focus areas

**Questions to ask in the interview:**
1. How does the Hong Kong team collaborate with the broader APAC and global Core Business Solutions practice?
2. What does success look like for an Advisory Solution Consultant in the first 12 months, especially for Core Business Solutions (HR, legal, procurement, finance)?
3. How does ServiceNow support remote or hybrid work for Hong Kong-based consultants, and what is the typical mix of local vs. regional engagements?

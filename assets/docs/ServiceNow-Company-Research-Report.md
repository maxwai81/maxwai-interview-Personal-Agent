# Company Research Report: ServiceNow

*Applying for Advisory Solution Consultant, Core Business Solutions*

---

## Executive Summary

ServiceNow is a leading enterprise workflow and AI platform (~$13B+ revenue, ~$120B market cap) that automates IT operations, field service, and business workflows across HR, Legal, Finance, and Procurement. The company is in strong growth mode (~21–24% YoY) with a sharp focus on agentic AI and GenAI. The **Advisory Solution Consultant, Core Business Solutions** role is a senior pre-sales position focused on HR, Legal, Finance, Procurement, and supplier management—requiring executive demos, discovery, solution design, RFP/POC leadership, and mentoring. ServiceNow values storytelling, simplification of complex concepts, and alignment with its customer-centric, team-oriented culture.

## Company Background

### Mission & Values
- **Mission:** "Making the world work better for everyone"
- **Tagline:** "The world works with ServiceNow™" / "Put AI to Work"
- **Value proposition:** Cloud-based enterprise software platform that unifies tools, systems, and data to automate workflows across the enterprise

### History & Founding
- **Founded:** November 5, 2003 (as Glidesoft, Inc.); incorporated in California 2004
- **Founder:** Fred Luddy (with David Loo, Don Goodliffe, Bow Ruggeri, Patrick Casey)
- **Origin:** Started with a single laptop at Luddy's desk in San Diego, California
- **IPO:** June 29, 2012 — raised $209.7M at ~$2.17B valuation
- **HQ:** Santa Clara, California

### Founding Vision
Three core insights drove the founding: (1) simplified platform for business applications, (2) browser-based delivery (novel in 2004), (3) SaaS vs. on-premise. Initial focus was ITSM to demonstrate platform capabilities. Early culture emphasized humility, integrity, customer-first thinking, and open communication.

- **Industry:** Enterprise software, cloud-based workflow automation
- **Employees:** ~25,000–29,000

- **Revenue growth:** ~21–24% YoY
- **Market cap:** ~$118–122B (NYSE: NOW)
- **Specialties:** IT Service Management, workflow automation, enterprise cloud

## Products & Services

### Core Business Solutions (Primary Focus for This Role)

The **Core Business Suite** connects employees, suppliers, systems, and data in a single suite. Domains include:
- **HR** — Employee service delivery, case management
- **Workplace** — Workplace services and experience
- **Legal** — Legal operations and matter management
- **Finance** — Finance operations and close management
- **Procurement** — Procurement and sourcing
- **Supplier management** — Supplier lifecycle and performance

**Key capabilities:**
- Case and process management across business functions
- Task orchestration to route work to the right experts
- Unified workspaces for employees
- Dashboard completion to track SLAs and operational analytics

### ITSM (IT Service Management)
- Automates core IT processes; manages incidents, problems, and changes
- Aligns with ITIL standards
- Manages access, availability, service requests, and service delivery

### ITOM (IT Operations Management)
- AIOps, insights, and automation to predict issues and streamline resolutions
- Proactive digital operations

### ITBM (IT Business Management)
- IT business management and planning

### Platform
All products run on the **ServiceNow AI Platform** with AI agents for automation, productivity, and 24/7 support. Core Business Solutions focuses on non-IT business workflows; ITSM/ITOM/ITBM focus on IT operations and planning.

## Executive Summary

ServiceNow is a leading enterprise workflow and AI platform (~$13B+ revenue, ~$120B market cap) that automates IT operations, field service, and business workflows across HR, Legal, Finance, Procurement, and Workplace. The company is in strong growth mode (~21–24% YoY) with a sharp focus on agentic AI and GenAI. The **Solution Sales Executive, Core Business Solutions (Hong Kong)** role is a **quota-carrying sales position** based in **Causeway Bay** (Hysan Place), focused on expanding ServiceNow beyond IT into enterprise-wide workflows—HR Service Delivery, Procurement, Finance Operations, Legal Contract Management, Workplace Service Delivery—across North Asia and the Philippines. The role requires 7+ years enterprise software sales, knowledge of Workday/SAP SuccessFactors/Oracle HCM/Coupa/Ariba, and business process expertise. ServiceNow has a North Asia HQ in Hong Kong (established 2013), 6 open Hong Kong roles, and hosts AI Summits in Hong Kong (2025, 2026). Glassdoor rates the company 4.2/5 with 93% CEO approval; sales culture is strong with high quota attainment and competitive OTE.

- **Workflow-first** — not ERP replacement; connects and automates across systems
- **Fast deployment** — Core Business Suite often available within weeks
- **Integration:** ServiceNow S2P + SAP Ariba "Better together" — ServiceNow front-end + Ariba back-end

## 11. Recommendation

**Verdict:** **Good** — ServiceNow is a strong employer for a Solution Sales Executive in Hong Kong. The company has market leadership, robust growth, a clear investment in AI and Core Business Solutions, and a dedicated North Asia HQ in Causeway Bay. The role aligns well with candidates who have enterprise software sales experience, domain knowledge in HR/Finance/Procurement/Legal, and familiarity with Workday, SAP, Oracle, Coupa, or Ariba.

### Key Factors
- **Strong financials:** ~$13B revenue, ~22% YoY growth, ~$120B market cap; GenAI momentum; Moveworks acquisition strengthens AI story
- **Hong Kong presence:** North Asia HQ in Causeway Bay (Hysan Place); established 2013; territory covers HK, Macau, Taiwan, Philippines
- **Role fit:** Solution Sales Executive is a specialist role for Core Business Solutions—expanding ServiceNow beyond IT; clear differentiation vs competitors
- **Culture:** 4.2/5 Glassdoor, 93% CEO approval; sales-led culture; high quota attainment; Fortune 100 Best Companies to Work For 2025
- **Compensation:** Competitive OTE for sales; US data suggests $210K+ OTE for AEs; HK/APAC likely similar structure with local adjustments

# Company Research Report: Qualtrics

*Applying for Head of Sales - Greater China (Hong Kong SAR)*

---

## Executive Summary

Qualtrics is a category-defining Experience Management (XM) platform serving 20,000+ brands across 100+ countries. Founded in 2002 in Provo, Utah, the company pioneered the XM space—combining customer, employee, product, and brand experience in a single platform. After an $8B acquisition by SAP (2018), IPO (2021), and $12.5B Silver Lake buyout (2023), Qualtrics is now privately held with strong AI investment and ~50% of customers on new AI capabilities. The **Head of Sales - Greater China (Hong Kong SAR)** role is a senior commercial leadership position to build and scale the Greater China sales organization, with Hong Kong as the hub for mainland China and Taiwan. APAC expansion is active (~1,200 hires planned by end of 2024), with Singapore, Tokyo, and Sydney as established offices; Hong Kong is a target market with customer presence and regional events. The role offers greenfield opportunity but requires navigating a demanding sales culture and potential structural ambiguity around Hong Kong office presence.

## Recommendation

**Verdict:** **Proceed with caution** — Qualtrics is a category leader in XM with strong backing, AI investment, and APAC expansion momentum. The Head of Sales - Greater China role offers significant greenfield opportunity to build a regional sales organization. However, the role may already be filled, Hong Kong office presence is less established than Singapore/Tokyo, and Glassdoor ratings (especially senior management at 2.6/5 and sales quota difficulty) warrant due diligence.

**Key factors:**
- **Category leadership:** XM platform; 20,000+ brands; Gartner VoC leader; AI roadmap
- **New CEO:** Jason Maynard brings strong enterprise sales and revenue operations background (Oracle/NetSuite)
- **APAC expansion:** ~1,200 hires planned; Singapore data centre; Center for Experience Management in Singapore
- **Greater China:** Active market; Hong Kong events and customers; role may be filled or in early build
- **Culture:** TACOS values; "scrappy," high-growth; sales quotas demanding; 57% recommend vs. industry average

**Questions to ask in the interview:**
1. What is the current state of the Greater China sales organization, and how does this role fit into the reporting structure (Head of Asia, regional APAC)?
2. What are the revenue targets and growth expectations for Greater China in the next 12–24 months, and what resources (SDR, SE, CSM) will support this role?
3. How does Qualtrics approach data sovereignty and compliance for mainland China customers, given the Singapore data centre?
4. What is the typical sales cycle and deal size for enterprise XM in Greater China, and which verticals (financial services, retail, travel) are prioritized?
5. What is the current Hong Kong office setup—remote, hybrid, or co-working—and what are the plans for physical presence in Hong Kong?
